Please carefully read the following information.

This is an experimental research study conducted by Dr. Gregory Chernov from University College London (UCL), approved by the German Association for Experimental Economic Research (Ethics approval number: uFbUXZ7f).
To consent to participate, you MUST meet the following criteria:

1) 18 years of age or older
2) Fluent speaker of English
3) Have not participated in this experiment before.

Your participation in this research is voluntary. You may refrain from answering any questions that make you uncomfortable and may withdraw your participation at any time without penalty by exiting this task and alerting the experimenter. You may choose not to complete certain parts of the task or answer certain questions. You may contact us at the address provided below if you have additional questions or concerns. Other than monetary compensation, participating in this study will provide no direct benefits to you. However, we hope that this research will benefit society at large by contributing towards establishing a scientific foundation for improving people’s decision-making.

If you have any questions about the study, feel free to contact project lead Gregory Chernov via gregory[at]daad-alumni.de

Consent: This information sheet provides information to help you decide whether to participate in this research study. Please read it carefully. Should you have any questions, please contact the investigators listed above. You can withdraw anytime, and your data will not be used.

Procedures and Purpose: In this study, you will be asked to make choices that will help researchers understand decision-making. This study is conducted under a strict policy of no deception; you will never be lied to during this study. Your responses will be used as part of an academic research project.

Benefits and Risks: There are no direct benefits associated with participating in this study. The results will be used for better understanding human behaviour. There are no risks to participating in this study.

Compensation: You will be compensated for your time; part of that compensation may depend on your answers.

Participation: Your participation in this study is voluntary. You may refuse to participate or withdraw from the study at any time without jeopardizing your employment, status, or any other entitlements. The investigators may withdraw you at their professional discretion.

Alternatives to Participation: The alternative to participate is to choose not to participate.

Confidentiality and Data Protection: The data collected for this study will be anonymised following the payment of compensation. All data and published work resulting from this experiment will maintain your individual privacy.

Expectations regarding Participants’ Answers: The investigators do not have specific expectations about participants' behaviour. Any choice made in line with the instructions will be recorded and considered valid.
